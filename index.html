New Monuments | Chicago was presented by Black Cube Nomadic Art Museum in association with the Chicago Park District. Architectural Intervention by AIM Architecture. Part of EXPO CHICAGO’s IN/SITU Outside program.

Made possible with support from: Canada Council for the Arts, Chicago Park District, David and Laura Merage Foundation, Project&’s Monuments to Movements, and The Andy Warhol Foundation for the Visual Arts.

Special thanks to: 21c Museum Hotel Chicago and EXPO CHICAGO.

As an invitation to participate in Brendan Fernandes’ project New Monuments | Chicago, we asked you and yours to respond to the following prompt: “How can we become the monument?” Answers were collected both virtually and in-person, and written on custom tags. These tags were displayed on-site at the Logan Monument from April 10–13, 2024.

- Feel your feelings
